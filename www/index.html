This is a simple application where two people can deploy and sign a legal contract on the Ethereum network without the need for a 3rd party. Once the contract has been signed by both parties, it cannot be altered and will forever exist on the Ethereum blockchain. A contract is only updatable by the two addresses included in the contract. The contract gets forever locked after both sides are in signed status. A contract document can only be uploaded when both parties are in Negotiatiating status. The contract document file is stored in IPFS(Infura).
